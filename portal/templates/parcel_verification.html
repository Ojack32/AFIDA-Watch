Parcel Verification & Site-Visit Enablement

Separate staffed, insured, credentialed service for verified parcel locations.

AFIDA Parcel Id is not a dispatchable location. AFIDA-Watch provides county/state triage. For site visits, field operations, or enforcement referrals, you need verified parcel geometry and assessor evidence.

This service converts high-tier AFIDA records into verified parcel locations with full chain of custody.

What We Verify

  • AFIDA Parcel Id → County APN / Tax Map ID
  • County APN → Verified parcel geometry (polygon or centroid)
  • Geometry → Assessor/recorder evidence links
  • All with VerificationConfidence rating (High/Medium/Low)
